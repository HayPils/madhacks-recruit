Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What is a hackathon?
    2. Madhacks is Wisconsin biggest student-run hackathon. Every year, we bring together hackers all across United States and Canada for a special weekend of coding, networking and fun.

  2. Do I need to be a computer science major?
    3. Absolutely Not! We are hiring students from all majors. You do not need to have any hackathon experience either. If you are passionate about hackathon or event planning, don't hesitant to apply.

  3. How much time commitment?
    4. We held weekly meeting for our team members, and usually we don't get busy until the week before the event. So the time commitment is very low.

  4. Who can apply?
    5. Any UW-Madison students are eligible to apply. We take students from all majors!

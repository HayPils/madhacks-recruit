Team Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What is a hackathon?
    2. Hackathon is an event where students gather together and collaborate intensively on a software or hardware project for 24 or 36 hours. The goal is to give students the opportunity to build the product that they've always wanted to do but never have the opportunity to do.

  2. Do I need to be a computer science major to join the team?
    3. Absolutely Not! We are hiring students from all majors. You do not need to have any hackathon experience either. If you are passionate about hackathon or event planning, please don't hesitant to apply.

  3. What is the time commitment?
    4. We held weekly meeting for our team organizers, and usually we don't get busy until the week or two before the event.

  4. What if I never been to a hackathon?
    5. No worries. As long as you are passionate about what you are doing, we can on board members pretty quickly so that they know how hackathon works.

  5. Can I still hack if I am part of the organized team?
    6. According to the MLH rule, unfortunately organized team are not allowed to hack. But we will still have monthly club events that will give you an opportunity to hack!

  6. What if I am not a student?
    7. If you are not a student and still wants to help out, send us an email at team@madhacks.org and let us know!

  7. What if I don't know what team to join?
    8. That's totally okay. We will give you more details about each team, as well as introducing you to the coordinator of the team you are interested in to give you a better idea of what each team does.
